# WINTR

A framework for building products without blind spots.

## Tagline

WIN Through Reasoning.

## Problem (Disease)

Jumping in too quickly and risking it all — building something doomed to fail because of gaps in expertise.

## Buyer

1. Technical PMs — building products for clients, want to be top 1%
2. Indie-hackers — building side-hustles with AI, want to avoid failure

Both share the same fear: wasting effort on something that was never going to work.

## Cure (Core Insight)

The trifecta eliminates blind spots:

- **UX** — user needs, proposition clarity
- **Business** — feasibility, value, funding
- **Code** — software designed to change

Experts in one dimension miss the details that cause failure. Only the trifecta covers all angles.

## Transformation

- **Functional:** "I shipped something of high quality"
- **Emotional:** "I feel furbo" — confident, like a professional who knows what they're doing

The guide delivers both: functional success drives emotional confidence.
